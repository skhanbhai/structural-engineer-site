Overview / About

A practice built around responsiveness, clarity, and buildable solutions.

Panoptic Design Consulting is led by Vijay Mehta. It’s a multi-disciplinary residential consultancy covering structural engineering, architectural design, and approvals. Ten years in, the feedback is remarkably consistent: quick to respond, easy to communicate with, and creative when a project doesn’t fit the standard detail.

About the practice

We deliver structural, architectural, and civil design for residential projects across London and the South East. The recurring feedback from clients, builders and architects who’ve worked with us is simple: responsive, clear, and practical.

The practice has been running for over a decade, led throughout by Vijay Mehta. In that time we’ve delivered more than 500 residential projects: extensions, loft conversions, wall removals, refurbishments, structural reports, and a smaller number of new builds and listed-building consents. Most enquiries come from past clients and the builders and architects we’ve worked with before.

What separates us from a typical structural engineering firm is that we can also handle the architecture and the approvals. For most domestic projects that’s three consultants reduced to one: a cleaner brief, coordinated drawings, and nobody to blame when something doesn’t add up on site because everything came out of the same studio.

For larger projects, or ones where you already have an architect you want to keep, we drop in as the structural consultant only. We use your naming conventions, hit your milestones, and stay out of decisions that aren’t ours to make. We’re not territorial about scope. We care about the outcome.

Section / 02 How we work

The six things clients keep telling us we do well.

These aren’t aspirations. They’re the themes that come up unprompted across 40+ Google reviews, on every kind of residential project from simple wall removals to listed-building consents.

P / 01

We answer the phone

The most common thing clients say about us is that we pick up, reply quickly, and book site visits within days. It’s embarrassingly rare for a consultancy. We’ve built the practice around making sure it stays true.

P / 02

We explain things clearly

Plain language for homeowners, technical detail for builders, and the right level of rigour for Building Control. You should always know what you’re paying for and why.

P / 03

We solve problems creatively

When the standard detail won’t work because of awkward geometry, tight budget, listed status or a neighbour dispute, we design around the problem rather than through it.

P / 04

We draw for the builder

Steel suppliers, site managers and architects consistently tell us our packs are easy to price and easy to build. That’s not an accident. Drawings exist to be installed, not admired.

P / 05

We price fairly and fixed

If we can define the scope, we quote a fixed fee. Competitive, transparent, and honoured even when a project takes longer than expected.

P / 06

We work alongside your team

We collaborate well with architects, builders and Building Control. That’s why architects bring us in on their schemes and why builders recommend us to their clients.

Section / 03 Clients

Who we work with.

Residential-focused, but not all residential clients look the same. Here’s who typically comes to us, and how the engagement looks for each.

01

Homeowners

The largest share. Extensions, loft conversions, and refurbishments on your own home. Usually we handle architecture, structure, and approvals end-to-end.

02

Small developers

Single-plot and small multi-unit developers who need a fast, commercially-minded consultancy. We work to programme and report clearly to stakeholders.

03

Architects

Practices who need a reliable structural engineer to work alongside their design. We integrate into your workflow, use your naming conventions, and don’t overreach.

04

Contractors

Builders who’ve encountered a structural problem mid-job, or who want a consultant on-call for site queries. Quick turnaround on site visits and reports.
