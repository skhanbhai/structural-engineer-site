Overview / About

A residential structural and design consultancy, based in London.

Panoptic Design Consulting is a multi-disciplinary practice covering structural engineering, architectural design, and approvals. We work with homeowners, architects and contractors across London and the South East. Most enquiries come through client recommendations and repeat engagements from people we’ve worked with before.

About the practice

We deliver structural, architectural, and civil design for residential projects. Practical, efficient design that balances cost, performance, and buildability, delivered by one coordinated team from concept to construction.

The practice covers extensions, loft conversions, wall removals, refurbishments, structural reports, and a smaller number of new builds and listed-building consents. Our work spans simple beam calculations through to multi-stage design packages requiring close coordination with architects, contractors, and Building Control bodies.

What distinguishes Panoptic from a typical structural engineering firm is the combined in-house capability. For most residential projects, three separate consultants become one coordinated team: a cleaner brief, a consistent drawing set, and no handoff gaps for your builder to absorb on site. It also removes the compounding fees and duplicated meetings of appointing separate disciplines.

For larger projects, or where you already have an architect you want to keep, we drop in as the structural consultant only. We use your naming conventions, hit your milestones, and stay out of decisions that aren’t ours to make. The aim is always outcome, not territory.

The practice is led by Vijay Mehta, an experienced chartered structural engineer focused on residential projects. Specialist input for surveys, party walls, MEP and acoustics is brought in through a tested network of collaborators.

Section / 02 How we work

How the practice operates.

Six commitments that govern how we scope, price, and deliver every project. They’re how Panoptic stays a small practice with the reliability of a larger firm.

P / 01

Scoped quotes within 48 hours

Most enquiries receive a scoped fee proposal within 48 hours of first contact, covering likely approvals route, fee structure, and expected timeline. Site visits are booked within one to two weeks.

P / 02

End-to-end project delivery

We support projects from feasibility through concept, planning, technical design, building control, tender, and construction-stage queries. Clients get one consultant moving the project forward rather than a chain of handoffs.

P / 03

Buildable, value-engineered design

Practical, efficient design that balances cost, performance, and buildability. Drawings that contractors can price without assumptions, and standard details that install on site without rework.

P / 04

Approvals handled with authority

Regular engagement with local authorities, Building Control bodies, and asset owners such as Thames Water. Packages are detailed and referenced to land approvals the first time.

P / 05

One coordinated fee

Structural engineering, architecture and approvals delivered under a single coordinated fee. No duplicated meetings, no contradictory drawings, and no commercial ambiguity when scope changes.

P / 06

Integrated with your team

Working closely with architects, builders, surveyors and contract administrators. We use your naming conventions, attend your design meetings, and stay out of decisions outside our remit.

Section / 03 Clients

Who we work with.

Residential-focused, but not all residential clients look the same. Here’s who typically comes to us, and how the engagement looks for each.

01

Homeowners

The largest share. Extensions, loft conversions, and refurbishments on your own home. Usually we handle architecture, structure, and approvals end-to-end.

02

Small developers

Single-plot and small multi-unit developers who need a fast, commercially-minded consultancy. We work to programme and report clearly to stakeholders.

03

Architects

Practices who need a reliable structural engineer to work alongside their design. We integrate into your workflow, use your naming conventions, and don’t overreach.

04

Contractors

Builders who’ve encountered a structural problem mid-job, or who want a consultant on-call for site queries. Quick turnaround on site visits and reports.
