Overview / About

A consultancy where the engineer, architect, and approvals run on the same drawing.

Panoptic Design Consulting was founded to close the gap that sits between structural engineering, architectural design, and approvals. Too many residential projects fail quietly in the handoffs - we remove them.

About the practice

We deliver structural, architectural, and civil design for residential projects across London and the South East - with a focus on clarity, approvals, and buildability.

The practice has been running for over a decade. In that time we’ve delivered more than 500 residential projects - extensions, loft conversions, refurbishments, structural reports, and a smaller number of new builds. Most of our work comes from past clients, the builders we’ve worked with, and the architects who’ve come to trust our calcs.

The thing that separates us from a typical structural engineering firm is that we can also handle the architecture and the approvals. For most domestic projects, that’s three consultants reduced to one - a cleaner brief, coordinated drawings, and no finger-pointing when something doesn’t add up.

For larger projects, or ones where you already have an architect you want to keep, we’re happy to drop in as the structural consultant only. We’re not territorial about the scope. We care about the outcome.

Section / 02 How we work

Six principles we don’t compromise on.

These aren’t values on a wall. They’re the decisions we make every week about how we scope, price, and deliver work - and they’re why clients come back.

P / 01

Clear communication, always

You’ll never wait three weeks for a reply. Every enquiry gets an answer the same day, every RFI within 24 hours.

P / 02

Drawings you can build from

Our drawings are detailed enough that contractors can price them without assumptions. It makes the tender fair and the build smoother.

P / 03

Approvals that land first time

We don’t submit until it’s right. That means longer prep, shorter approvals, and far fewer rejections to argue through.

P / 04

Practical, not pretty

Buildability beats cleverness. We’d rather deliver a standard detail that the builder knows how to install than a novel one that stalls on site.

P / 05

Fixed fees wherever possible

If we can define the scope, we quote a fixed fee. No scope creep, no surprise invoices, no stage-end negotiation.

P / 06

Independent, always

We don’t take commissions from builders, steel suppliers, or window companies. Our advice is yours, and ours alone.

Section / 03 Clients

Who we work with.

Residential-focused, but not all residential clients look the same. Here’s who typically comes to us, and how the engagement looks for each.

01

Homeowners

The largest share. Extensions, loft conversions, and refurbishments on your own home. Usually we handle architecture, structure, and approvals end-to-end.

02

Small developers

Single-plot and small multi-unit developers who need a fast, commercially-minded consultancy. We work to programme and report clearly to stakeholders.

03

Architects

Practices who need a reliable structural engineer to work alongside their design. We integrate into your workflow, use your naming conventions, and don’t overreach.

04

Contractors

Builders who’ve encountered a structural problem mid-job, or who want a consultant on-call for site queries. Quick turnaround on site visits and reports.
