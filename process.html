Overview / Process

How we work. Step by step, with the phone always answered.

Every project runs through the same six stages. At every handover you’ll know what’s been done, what’s needed from you, and when the next deliverable is due. No silent weeks. No chasing.

01

Initial consultation

Duration · 30 minutes · Free

A call - or a short site walk if you’re local - to understand the property, your brief, and what you’re trying to achieve. We’ll talk you through what’s realistic, flag anything unusual, and confirm whether we’re the right consultancy for the project.

You’ll come out of this with a clear view of the likely scope, approvals route, and order of magnitude cost - before you commit to any fees.

What you leave with
  • Scope summary
  • Approvals plan
  • Cost range
  • Fee proposal (within 48h)
02

Site assessment

Duration · 1–2 hours on site · Within 2 weeks

Once appointed, we visit the property to take a full measured survey and assess the existing structure. We photograph every room, record dimensions, note any existing defects, and investigate the visible structural elements - load-bearing walls, beams, floor build-ups, and roof structure.

If there are things we need to see that aren’t accessible (a loft void, a basement, under a floor), we agree how and when to revisit with the builder.

Deliverables
  • Existing floor plans
  • Elevations and sections
  • Structural notes
  • Site photo record
03

Structural design

Duration · 2–4 weeks

We develop the structural concept alongside the architectural layout - so load paths, beam depths, and column positions are resolved before they become aesthetic problems. Steels are sized, foundations are specified, and we check the design against both the building regulations and what’s practical to build.

Where the project needs it, we iterate with you on layout, openings, and materials. Our aim is to land at a design that’s approvable, affordable, and buildable.

Deliverables
  • Concept drawings
  • Structural scheme
  • Beam & foundation sizes
  • Design review
04

Drawings & calculations

Duration · 2–3 weeks

The full drawing and calculation package, developed for approvals and for the contractor to price. Signed and stamped structural calculations, steel specs, foundation details, and coordinated architectural drawings at a level of detail that removes ambiguity on site.

This is the package that separates a project that runs smoothly from one that doesn’t. We don’t cut corners here.

Deliverables
  • Stamped calcs
  • Construction drawings
  • Details pack
  • Tender-issue set
05

Building control & approvals

Duration · 3–8 weeks · varies by authority

We submit the full-plans application to Building Control - either the local authority or an approved inspector - and handle the queries. Where the project needs planning, PD, party wall, or conservation consent, we drive that process too.

The goal is a clean approval the first time. If a query comes back, we respond within 48 hours and keep the approvals moving rather than letting them sit in a queue.

What we handle
  • BR submission
  • Planning / PD / CLP
  • Party Wall coordination
  • Technical queries
06

Support during build

Duration · Throughout construction

Once you’re on site, we remain available for site visits, RFIs, and design clarifications. For key structural sequences - underpinning, beam installation, foundation pours - we attend in person to confirm the work matches the design before it gets buried.

Our standing rule: RFIs get a response within 24 hours. If your builder is waiting on us, the build is paused, and that costs you money. We don’t let that happen.

What’s included
  • Key-stage site visits
  • 24h RFI turnaround
  • Design variations
  • Completion sign-off
Appendix / A Fees

How we price work.

We use fixed fees wherever the scope is clear enough to do so. You’ll know the total cost before we start, and any change in scope triggers a clearly-priced variation - not a mystery invoice at the end.

F / 01

Fixed fees

For well-defined scopes - calcs packages, structural reports, planning applications - we quote a single fixed fee including one round of revisions.

Most common
F / 02

Stage-gated fees

For larger projects, we split the fee across RIBA-style stages: concept, developed design, technical design, construction support. You commit stage by stage.

Extensions + new builds
F / 03

Hourly

Used only for investigative work where the scope genuinely can’t be defined upfront - cracking inspections, heritage research, or unusual party-wall matters.

Exception only
