Overview / Cookie policy

Cookie policy.

A short, plain-English summary of the cookies this site uses, and how to change your choice.

1. The short version

Panoptic Design uses two kinds of cookies. Essential cookies are needed for the site to work and the consent banner to remember your choice; they are always on. Analytics cookies let us see anonymously which pages people read so we can improve the site; they only run if you accept them on the banner. You can change your choice any time via the “Cookie settings” link in the footer.

2. Essential cookies

We use a single piece of localStorage on your device called panoptic-consent. It stores either “accepted” or “rejected” so we don’t show you the cookie banner on every visit. It is set in your browser, never sent to us, and you can clear it from your browser settings at any time.

3. Analytics cookies (optional)

If you accept analytics on the banner, we load Google Analytics 4 (GA4). GA4 sets a small number of cookies on your device, typically _ga and _ga_*, which it uses to count distinct visitors and sessions. It does not store any personally identifiable information from us; we do not use it for advertising; and we do not share the data with advertising networks.

If you reject analytics, GA4 is never loaded on your device for this site — no tracking script, no analytics cookies, no requests to googletagmanager.com or google-analytics.com.

4. Other things this site loads

Google Fonts. The site renders typography using fonts hosted on fonts.googleapis.com and fonts.gstatic.com. This is a one-off request to fetch the font files; it does not place a cookie on your device.

Cloudflare. The site is delivered via Cloudflare’s global CDN. Cloudflare may log IP addresses and request metadata at the edge for caching, security and abuse prevention. We do not have access to those logs as a marketing dataset.

5. Where enquiries go

When you submit the contact form or use the WhatsApp enquiry modal, the details you send are routed to a Google Apps Script web app under our Google Workspace account, which appends a row to a Google Sheet (“Panoptic Website Enquiries”) and, for form submissions, sends a notification email to info@panopticdesign.co.uk. See our privacy notice for the full picture.

6. Changing your mind

Click Cookie settings in the footer of any page to reopen the banner and switch your choice. Clearing site data in your browser also resets it.
