Overview / Privacy notice

Privacy notice.

A short, plain-English summary of what we collect when you contact us, how we use it, and how to ask us to delete it. Aligned with the UK GDPR and Data Protection Act 2018.

1. Who we are

This site is run by Panoptic Design Consulting Ltd, a private limited company registered in England. References to “we”, “us” or “our practice” mean the company. You can reach us at info@panopticdesign.co.uk or on 07854 598136.

2. What we collect

When you submit the enquiry form, we collect the information you provide: your name, email address, telephone number (if given), property postcode, the type of project, and the description you write. We also record the page you submitted from and any UTM parameters in the URL so we know which channel led you here.

We do not collect special-category data or payment details. We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to understand which pages people read and how they reach the site, with IP addresses anonymised by default and no advertising identifiers enabled. The data is held in our own GA4 property under our Google account; we do not share it with advertising networks.

3. Why we use it

We use what you send us only to reply to your enquiry, scope the project, and stay in touch about that specific piece of work. The lawful basis is “legitimate interests” (responding to a request you initiated), and where you tick the consent box, “consent”.

We do not use your details for marketing, do not pass them to third parties for marketing, and do not add you to any mailing list.

4. How it’s sent and stored

Submissions are sent to a Google Apps Script Web App that we control. The script appends a row to a Google Sheet (“Panoptic Website Enquiries”) that holds enquiry records and, where applicable, sends a notification email to our inbox. Both the sheet and the script run inside Google Workspace under our account, hosted in Google’s data centres in the EU/UK or under standard contractual clauses.

The site itself is hosted on Cloudflare, which delivers the pages over its global CDN. Cloudflare may log IP addresses and request metadata at the edge for caching, security and abuse-prevention purposes. We do not have access to those logs as a marketing dataset.

If you send us photos or drawings, we store them only for as long as we are working with you, plus a reasonable record-retention period afterwards (typically up to six years for project records, in line with normal professional practice).

5. Cookies

On your first visit you’ll see a consent banner with two buttons — “Accept analytics” or “Reject analytics”. Your choice is stored in your browser only (localStorage key panoptic-consent) so we don’t ask again on every visit. You can change your choice any time via the Cookie settings link in the footer.

If you accept, Google Analytics 4 loads and sets a small number of analytics cookies (typically _ga and _ga_*) which count distinct visitors and sessions. They do not store personally identifiable data and are not used for advertising. If you reject, GA4 is never loaded for this site — no tracking script, no analytics cookies, no requests to googletagmanager.com or google-analytics.com.

Fonts are loaded from Google Fonts; that request is needed to render the page and does not place a cookie on your device. See the full cookie policy for details.

6. Your rights

Under UK GDPR you can ask to see what we hold about you, correct it, delete it, or restrict how we use it. Email info@panopticdesign.co.uk with the subject “Data request” and we’ll respond within 30 days. You can also complain to the Information Commissioner’s Office if you think we’ve handled your data badly.

7. Changes

If we update this notice we’ll change the “last updated” date below. Material changes will be flagged at the top of the page for at least a month.
