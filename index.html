Section / 05 Process

A clear path from enquiry to completed build.

Every project runs through the same six stages. You always know where we are, what’s needed from you, and what’s coming next. No silent weeks, no guessing when the next deliverable lands.

01 - Consultation

Initial consultation

A free call to understand what you’re trying to achieve, the constraints of your property, and what’s realistic within your budget.

02 - Assessment

Site assessment

We visit the property, take measurements, and assess the existing structure - typically within 1–2 weeks of enquiry.

03 - Design

Structural design

Calculations, steel sizing and foundation design, developed alongside the architectural layout so nothing conflicts on site.

04 - Drawings

Drawings & calculations

Signed, stamped structural drawings and calculations, plus coordinated architectural packages ready for pricing and approvals.

05 - Approvals

Building control

We submit to Building Control and planning where needed, respond to technical queries, and keep the approvals moving.

06 - On site

Support during build

Site visits, RFIs, and design clarifications during construction - with queries responded to promptly where build-stage support is included.
