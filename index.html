Section / 05 Process

A clear path from enquiry to completed build.

Every project runs through the same six stages. You always know where we are, what’s needed from you, and what’s coming next. No silent weeks, no guessing when the next deliverable lands.

01 - Consultation

Initial consultation

A free call to understand what you’re trying to achieve, the constraints of your property, and what’s realistic within your budget.

02 - Assessment

Site assessment

We visit the property, take measurements, and assess the existing structure - typically within 1–2 weeks of enquiry.

03 - Design

Structural design

Calculations, steel sizing and foundation design, developed alongside the architectural layout so nothing conflicts on site.

04 - Drawings

Drawings & calculations

Signed, stamped structural drawings and calculations, plus coordinated architectural packages ready for pricing and approvals.

05 - Approvals

Building control

We submit to Building Control and planning where needed, respond to technical queries, and keep the approvals moving.

06 - On site

Support during build

Site visits, RFIs, and design clarifications during construction - so the builder always has an answer within 24 hours.

Section / 06 Most common enquiries

Projects we’ve done dozens of.

If your project sounds like one of these, we’ve probably done a version of it this year. Most enquiries can be scoped on a 15-minute call: cost range, approvals route, timeline.

Removing load-bearing walls

Opening up kitchens and ground-floor living spaces. Steel beam sizing, padstone design, and temporary works advice, all in one pack. Steel suppliers tend to call them “second to none”.

Typical turnaround · 2 weeks

Loft conversions

Dormers, hip-to-gables, mansards and L-shapes. Steel beams, posi-joists, dormer framing, building-control liaison. One of our most repeated project types.

Typical turnaround · 3–4 weeks

Structural reports for purchases

Buying a property and worried about cracks, movement or past alterations? We visit, inspect, and write a report your solicitor can actually use. Fast turnaround when timelines are tight.

Typical turnaround · 5 days

Cracking, chimneys and wall concerns

Investigation and diagnosis of cracking, bowing walls, sloping floors and suspected subsidence. Chimney stack removals, breast removals, and neighbour-wall conversations if needed.

Typical turnaround · 1 week

Extensions needing calculations

Got an architect but need the structural calcs and drawings? We drop into the team, use your naming conventions, and deliver a pack that stands up at tender and on site.

Typical turnaround · 2–3 weeks

Garage conversions & refurbs

Garages turned into habitable rooms, whole-house refurbs, and layout reworks. Architecture, structure and approvals all drawn by the same team so nothing falls between stools.

Typical turnaround · 3–6 weeks
